Hotels

Mary Beach Hotel

A nicely situated, calm and affordable hotel located on the southern part of the island.

Created in 2006, Mary Beach Hotel welcomes all kinds of tourists from all arround the world! It has great room service, rooms are spaceous and perfectly decorated to put you in an adventurous mood for your stay.

Prices can range from $250/night to $1,560/night.

Jurassic World Resort

This classic Hotel will guarantee your utmost comfortable and luxurious stay on the island.

It is designed to be visually appealing for all tourists, and with a modern and technological touch to it you will feel like you are in a very advacned society.

Prices can range anywhere from $450/night to $6,780/night

Mini Coco Hotel

You will love the natural flavour of this hotel, it will bring you closer to nature and in touch with the dinos.

For starters, it has an amazing staff, grat room service and disciplined managers. Breakfeast is on the house, it contains amazing fruits and vegetables native to the island (and even a few ancient fruits!).

Prices can range anywhere from $150/night to $780/night
